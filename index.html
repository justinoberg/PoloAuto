Service One

MAINTAINING YOUR AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM MEANS THAT YOU ALWAYS HAVE ENOUGH FREON FOR BEST PERFORMANCE.

A small leak in the air conditioning system of the vehicle allows Freon to escape and the system to not cool the air correctly.

It is recommended that we do a check of your air conditioning system while in the Shop. Common problems related to an air conditioner that can break down are:

1) Belt tightness. 2) Blocked airflow through the condenser and radiator. 3) Visible coolant / oil stain on the compressor, hoses, pipes and accessories. 4) Faulty compressor, or loose mounting brackets. 5) Missing service port seals. That is why car manufacturers recommend that your air conditioning system be service and recharged with Freon and lubricant at least every 2 years or 40,000 miles. Try to do this before the start of the season’s hottest month and you will not be caught unprepared by a sudden heat wave. An air conditioning unit can be a breeding ground for bacteria and fungi. This is particularly important for those suffering from asthma or allergies. The evaporator cooling cabin air filter and the entire interior of the car should also be checked; a well functioning air conditioner should leave your car with a pleasant smell.
